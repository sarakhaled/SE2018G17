Hair loss

Some types of hair can be damaged due to damage to the skin of the scalp.

    Along with hair loss, have you noticed any of the following changes in the skin of your scalp?
  • redness
  • Peel
  • Irritation or scratching
  • fibrosis
  • Open ulcers
